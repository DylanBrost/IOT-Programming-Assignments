# IOT-Programming-Assignments

1. Ciruitverse_SR.mp4:
   Shift Register built in Circuitverse with 8 D Flip-Flops.

2. SR_TinkerCAD.MP4:
   Shows a video of a working shift register on tinkerCaD that moves bits of Data 1/0, indicated by the shifting LEDs.

3. Bill&tipCalculator.cs
   A C# Calculator for determining your bill amount with the added tip or your share of the bill when divided amoung others, Prints out invoices to txt file.

4. TODOlist.cs
   A to do list for organizing your tasks you need to complete with the ability to import and export them to a txt file.

5. TheLosttreasure.cs
   Text based adventure game with choice based desicions. Log status at each scene to a txt file.

6. shift register.pdf
   Schematic view of the shift register in the SR_TinkerCAD.MP4
